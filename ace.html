what are the main categories of swimming

    there are two main categories of it
  1. free swimming
  2. fins swimming

what are the categories of fins swimming

    fins swimming have two types
  1. is swimming with double fins in it we move our arms alternately:one arm pulls underwater while the other recovers over the water and kick our legs with quick,strong kicks from the hips both at the same time
  2. monofin swimming in it we use a single large fin instead of two seperate fins,your legs stay together and you move your entire body in a wave-like motion (from head to toes),there is no separate kicking with the legs like in normal swimming:it's a full body undulation

what are the categories of free swimming

    free swimming have four categories
  1. free swimming its like double fins style but with out fins its the same way exactly in it we breath from our side
  2. back stroke its like the free swimming and double fins but we swim by our back
  3. breaststroke in it both arms move outward and then sweep back together underneath your body, your legs preform a frog kick:pull your heels towards your body.then push them backward strongly your body stays close to the water surface most of the time you breathe when you lift your head up after the arm pull and kick breaststroke is slower than other strokes but very energy-efficient and comfortable.
  4. butterfly swimming in it both arms move together: you stretch them forward,then pull them down and back under your body your body creates a wave like motion with each stroke both legs kick together using a dolphin kick motion you breathe when you lift your head above the water during the arm recovery it requires strong coordination between the arms,legs,and body.
